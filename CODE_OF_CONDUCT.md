# Code of Conduct

Continuum follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

The short version: be kind, assume good faith, don't be a jerk. People are giving you their time for free.

## Enforcement

Reports go to **conduct@getcontinuum.dev**. Reports will be handled discreetly; reporters won't be named without their consent.

Consequences for a violation range from a private warning to a permanent ban from project spaces, at maintainer discretion. The maintainer is the final word.
